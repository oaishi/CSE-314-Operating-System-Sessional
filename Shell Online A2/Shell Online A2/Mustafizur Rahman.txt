Mustafizur Rahman
Bangladesh

Bowler

Mustafizur Rahman is a left-arm pace bowler who came to Dhaka to try out for a fast-bowling camp in 2012, 
after he had impressed in an Under-17 tournament in Satkhira, some 300km from Dhaka. He got admitted to the 
BCB's pace foundation and soon caught the coaches' attention to make the Bangladesh Under-19 side for the 2014 World Cup.
